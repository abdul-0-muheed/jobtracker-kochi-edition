How Sync Works
  • Scrapes your sent LinkedIn connection requests
  • Matches recipients to your tracked companies
  • Captures message threads with company employees
  • Rate-limited (8–15s delays between requests)
  • Max 30 page loads per sync session
  • Session encrypted with your passphrase (AES-256)
Important

LinkedIn scraping is read-only and operates within a real logged-in session — the same as using LinkedIn in your browser.

The app reads: your sent connection requests, your messages with company employees. It does NOT send connections or messages on your behalf.
