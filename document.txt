HOW PHASE 2 WORKS:
  - Queries Company.query.filter(career_page_url != NULL)
  - Calls scrape_career_page(url) for each company
  - Enriches job dict with company.name and company.company_type
  - Sets source = "{CompanyName} (Career Page)"  so you can filter by it in Jobs page
  - Saves to ScrapedJob (NOT Opening/CRM) — companies list stays clean
  - Scrapes concurrently/sequentially without the 3-10s random evasion delay used in Phase 1 
    (since these are all distinct domains, no IP ban risk from aggressive polling).
